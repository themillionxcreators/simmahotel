The Himalayan Table

A Culinary Journey Through the Mountains

Perched in the heart of Darjeeling, The Himalayan Table is more than a restaurant — it is a celebration of the region's rich culinary heritage. Our chefs draw inspiration from the misty hills, crafting dishes that honour traditional recipes while embracing contemporary techniques.

Every ingredient is thoughtfully sourced — from the freshest local produce to the finest Darjeeling tea leaves. The panoramic windows frame the majestic Kanchenjunga, making every meal a feast for the senses.

The Golden Bar

Where Mist Meets Mixology

Step into a world of understated luxury at The Golden Bar. Our bartenders are artists, crafting cocktails that are as visually stunning as they are delicious. From rare single malts to signature creations inspired by the Himalayas, every drink tells a story.

The warm ambiance, soft lighting, and panoramic mountain views create the perfect setting for an evening of conversation and celebration.
