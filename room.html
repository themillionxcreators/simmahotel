From our curated mountain-view rooms to our award-winning dining experiences, we craft moments that linger in your heart long after you leave.

Our Heritage

A Love Letter to Darjeeling

Simma Hotel was born from a deep reverence for the misty mountains and the warm spirit of Darjeeling. Our founders envisioned a sanctuary where travellers could experience the region's magic without compromising on world-class luxury.

We invite you to be part of our story, to create your own memories against the backdrop of the Kanchenjunga.
