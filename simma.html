About Simma

A Himalayan Sanctuary of Luxury

Nestled in the misty hills of Darjeeling, Simma Hotel Restaurant & Bar offers an unparalleled escape where the majesty of the Himalayas meets world-class hospitality. Every sunrise brings a new canvas of golden light over the Kanchenjunga peaks.

From our curated mountain-view rooms to our award-winning dining experiences, we craft moments that linger in your heart long after you leave.
